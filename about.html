About Bagicha

Bagicha (meaning "garden" in Hindi) is a free, open-source project that helps people discover beautiful plants, learn gardening tips, and build their own virtual garden — all from the comfort of their browser.

🌍

Our Mission

We believe that everyone should have access to gardening knowledge, regardless of their experience level. Bagicha aims to make plant discovery fun, educational, and accessible to all.

Whether you're a seasoned gardener or just starting out, Bagicha provides the tools and information you need to cultivate your green thumb.

⚡

Built with Simplicity

Bagicha is built entirely with HTML5, CSS3, and Vanilla JavaScript — no frameworks, no build tools, no dependencies.

This makes it fast, lightweight, and easy for beginners to understand, modify, and learn from.
